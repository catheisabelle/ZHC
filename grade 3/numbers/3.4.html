More means adding, and less means subtracting.
Finding 1 More/Less
1 More: To find 1 more, just add 1 to the number.

Example:

What is 1 more than 253?
253 + 1 = 254. So, 1 more than 253 is 254.


1 Less: To find 1 less, just subtract 1 from the number.

Example:

What is 1 less than 253?
253 - 1 = 252. So, 1 less than 253 is 252.
