Recite and read/write numbers up to 1000

Let’s Count from 1 to 1000!
Counting is the process of naming numbers in the correct order. You can count by 1s, 10s, or 100s to help with counting large numbers.
Counting by 1s
To count by 1s, start at 1 and keep adding 1 until you reach 1000.

Here’s how:

1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6... all the way up to 1000!

Counting by 10s
To count by 10s, start at 10 and keep adding 10.

Here’s how:

10, 20, 30, 40, 50... up to 1000!

Counting by 100s
To count by 100s, start at 100 and keep adding 100.

Here’s how:

100, 200, 300, 400... up to 1000!

Reading Large Numbers
When you see large numbers, you can read them like this:
  • 354 = three hundred fifty-four
  • 875 = eight hundred seventy-five
Practice reading these numbers out loud to get better at it!
