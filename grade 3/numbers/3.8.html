Find Halves, Thirds, Quarters, and Tenths

Let’s Learn About Fractions of Shapes and Numbers!
Fractions are parts of a whole. You can find fractions by dividing shapes or numbers into equal parts.
Finding Halves (½) of Shapes
Halves (½): To find a half, divide the shape into 2 equal parts.

Example: Cut a circle into 2 equal pieces. Each piece is a half.

Finding Half of a Number (½)
Finding half of a number: To find half of a number, divide it by 2.

Example: Half of 12 is 6 because 12 ÷ 2 = 6.

Finding Thirds (⅓) of Shapes
Thirds (⅓): To find thirds, divide the shape into 3 equal parts.

Example: Cut a rectangle into 3 equal sections. Each part is one-third.

Finding One-Third of a Number (⅓)
Finding one-third of a number: To find one-third of a number, divide it by 3.

Example: One-third of 15 is 5 because 15 ÷ 3 = 5.

Finding Quarters (¼) of Shapes
Quarters (¼): To find quarters, divide the shape into 4 equal parts.

Example: Divide a square into 4 equal sections. Each part is a quarter.

Finding One-Quarter of a Number (¼)
Finding one-quarter of a number: To find one-quarter of a number, divide the number by 4.

Example: One-quarter of 20 is 5 because 20 ÷ 4 = 5.

Finding Tenths (¹⁄₁₀) of Shapes
Tenths (¹⁄₁₀): To find tenths, divide the shape into 10 equal parts.

Example: If you draw 10 equal slices in a circle, each slice is one-tenth.

Finding One-Tenth of a Number (¹⁄₁₀)
Finding one-tenth of a number: To find one-tenth of a number, divide the number by 10.

Example: One-tenth of 50 is 5 because 50 ÷ 10 = 5.
