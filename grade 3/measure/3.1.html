Measuring is finding out how long, heavy, or full something is using the right tools.
Length Measurement
Length is how long or tall something is.
  • Units: Centimeters (cm) and Meters (m)
  • Tools: Use a ruler, measuring tape, or meter stick.
Example: Use a ruler to measure a pencil in centimeters.

Mass Measurement
Mass is how heavy something is.
  • Units: Grams (g) and Kilograms (kg)
  • Tools: Use a weighing scale.
Example: A loaf of bread weighs about 500 grams.

Capacity Measurement
Capacity is how much liquid fits in a container.
  • Units: Milliliters (ml) and Liters (l)
  • Tools: Use a measuring jug or cup.
Example: A small juice box holds 250 milliliters.
