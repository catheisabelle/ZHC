Multiply and Divide Numbers

Let’s Learn About Multiplication and Division!
Multiplication and division are both ways to work with numbers. Multiplication is a faster way to add, and division is a way to share things equally. Let's explore how to multiply and divide numbers.
Multiplying Single-Digit Numbers
Multiplication: Multiplication is a quick way to add the same number many times.

Example: If you have 3 groups of 4 apples, you can add 4 + 4 + 4, or just multiply: 3 × 4 = 12.

Multiplication Facts to Practice:
  • 2 × 6 = 12
  • 5 × 7 = 35
  • 9 × 4 = 36
Dividing Two-Digit Numbers
Division: Division means splitting a number into equal parts or groups.

Example: If you have 20 candies and share them with 5 friends, each friend gets 4 candies: 20 ÷ 5 = 4.

How to Divide Two-Digit Numbers:
Divide step by step and use the facts you know.

Example:
  • 20 ÷ 2 = 10 (Split 20 into 2 equal groups, each with 10.)
  • 36 ÷ 9 = 4 (Split 36 into 9 groups, each with 4.)
