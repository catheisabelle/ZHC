Recognize Equivalence Between Fractions, Decimals, and Percentages

Let’s learn how fractions, decimals, and percentages can represent the same values!
These are just different ways to show parts of a whole.
What Does Equivalence Mean?
  • Equivalence means that different forms of a number can represent the same value.
  • Fractions, decimals, and percentages are just different ways to show parts of a whole.
Fractions to Decimals
  • To convert a fraction to a decimal, divide the numerator (top number) by the denominator (bottom number).
Examples:
  • 1/2 = 1 ÷ 2 = 0.5
  • 3/4 = 3 ÷ 4 = 0.75
Decimals to Percentages
  • First, convert the fraction to a decimal, then multiply by 100.
Examples:
  • 1/2 = 0.5 × 100 = 50%
  • 3/4 = 0.75 × 100 = 75%
Equivalences to Remember
  • 1/2 = 0.5 = 50%
  • 1/4 = 0.25 = 25%
  • 3/4 = 0.75 = 75%
  • 1/10 = 0.1 = 10%
