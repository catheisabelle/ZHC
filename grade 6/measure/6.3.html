Understanding and Converting Time Units

Time Units
  • Seconds (s): The smallest unit of time.
  • Minutes (min): 1 minute = 60 seconds.
  • Hours (h): 1 hour = 60 minutes = 3600 seconds.
  • Days (d): 1 day = 24 hours.
  • Weeks (w): 1 week = 7 days.
  • Months (mo): The number of days in a month varies (28-31 days).
  • Years (yr): 1 year = 12 months = 365 or 366 days (leap year).
Converting Seconds to Minutes
60 seconds = 1 minute
Example:
Convert 120 seconds to minutes:
120 ÷ 60 = 2 minutes

Converting Minutes to Hours
60 minutes = 1 hour
Example:
Convert 150 minutes to hours:
150 ÷ 60 = 2.5 hours

Converting Hours to Days
24 hours = 1 day
Example:
Convert 48 hours to days:
48 ÷ 24 = 2 days

Converting Days to Weeks
7 days = 1 week
Example:
Convert 14 days to weeks:
14 ÷ 7 = 2 weeks

Converting Days to Hours
1 day = 24 hours
Example:
Convert 3 days to hours:
3 × 24 = 72 hours

Additional Time Conversions
  • 1 hour = 60 minutes = 3600 seconds
  • 1 day = 24 hours = 1440 minutes = 86400 seconds
  • 1 week = 7 days = 168 hours = 10080 minutes = 604800 seconds
