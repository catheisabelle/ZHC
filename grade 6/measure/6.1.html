Metric Units and Conversion

What Are Metric Units?
The metric system uses standard units of measurement for length, mass, and capacity. Common metric units include:
  • Length: kilometers (km), meters (m), centimeters (cm), millimeters (mm)
  • Mass: kilograms (kg), grams (g)
  • Capacity: liters (l), milliliters (ml)
Converting Between Units
Length Conversion:
  • 1 km = 1000 m
  • 1 m = 100 cm
  • 1 cm = 10 mm
Example:
Convert 2.5 meters to centimeters:
  • 2.5 m × 100 = 250 cm
Converting Between Units
Mass Conversion:
  • 1 kg = 1000 g
Example:
Convert 3.6 kg to grams:
  • 3.6 kg × 1000 = 3600 g
Converting Between Units
Capacity Conversion:
  • 1 l = 1000 ml
Example:
Convert 1.2 liters to milliliters:
  • 1.2 l × 1000 = 1200 ml
