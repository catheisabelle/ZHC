Understanding right angles

What is a right angle?
A right angle is an angle that looks like the corner of a square. It makes a 90° angle, which is a perfect L-shape.
How to recognize right angles:
  • Right angles are square corners. The corner of a book, a piece of paper, or a picture frame all have right angles.
  • Example: If you look at the corner of a square or rectangle, that’s a right angle!
What does a right angle look like?
  • A right angle looks like the letter L. It’s a sharp corner, not wide or small.
  • Right Angle Symbol: You can draw a small square at the corner to show it’s a right angle.
How to check for right angles:
Use your hands:
  • Make an L shape with your hands, like the corner of a square. That’s a right angle!

Check with a square:
  • Take a piece of paper and fold it into a square. All the corners of the square are right angles.
