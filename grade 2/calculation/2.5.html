Understand division as grouping, use ÷ sign

What is division?
Division means splitting things into equal groups or sharing them equally. We use the ÷ sign to show division.
How does division work?
Division can be thought of as two different things: sharing and grouping

Division as sharing:
  • If you have 12 candies and 4 friends, how many candies does each friend get?
  • Example: 12 ÷ 4 = 3 because each friend gets 3 candies.
How does division work?
Division can be thought of as two different things: sharing and grouping

Division as grouping:
  • If you have 12 candies and want to put them into groups of 4, how many groups can you make?
  • Example: 12 ÷ 4 = 3 because you can make 3 groups of 4.
What does each number mean?
Example: 10 ÷ 2 = 5
  • The 10 is the total number of things you have.
  • The 2 is the size of each group or the number of groups.
  • The 5 is the answer: the number of groups or how many each group gets.
