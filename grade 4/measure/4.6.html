Understand Area in Square Units

What is Area?
Area tells us how much space is inside a shape. For a rectangle or square, the area is how many square units (like tiny squares) fit inside the shape.
How to Find Area in Square Units
Understanding square units:
A square unit is a square that has equal sides, like a 1 cm by 1 cm square.

Counting squares:
To find the area of a shape like a rectangle, count how many squares fit inside.

Example: Finding the Area of a Rectangle
Rectangle with length = 4 squares and width = 3 squares:
Step 1: Count how many squares fit in: 4 squares long and 3 squares wide.
Step 2: Multiply the length and width: 4 Ã— 3 = 12 square units.

So, the area is 12 square units.
