Calculate Perimeters of Rectangles

What is a Perimeter?
The perimeter of a shape is the total distance around the outside of the shape. For a rectangle, the perimeter is the total length of all four sides.
How to Calculate the Perimeter of a Rectangle
Formula for perimeter:
Perimeter = 2 × (Length + Width)
This means you add the length and the width, then multiply the sum by 2 because there are two sides of each length and width.

Example: Calculating the Perimeter of a Rectangle
Rectangle with Length = 8 cm and Width = 5 cm:
Step 1: Add length and width: 8 cm + 5 cm = 13 cm
Step 2: Multiply by 2: 2 × 13 cm = 26 cm

So, the perimeter of this rectangle is 26 cm.
